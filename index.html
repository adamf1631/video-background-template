IS AIKIDO DIFFERENT FROM OTHER ARTS?

Aikido is not a sport like judo or kick-boxing nor, does it involve attacks like karate or kung-fu.
Aikido is strictly self-defense.
The people you train with are your practice partners, not your opponents. In Aikido physical relaxation is encouraged so there is a relaxed, friendly atmosphere.
